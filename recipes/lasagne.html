Beef lasagne

Beef lasagne is a flat and expanded pasta sheet, traditionally made in Italy with Parmigiano-Reggiano (Parmesan cheese), Béchamel sauce (white sauce), and ragù (a meat-based sauce).

Ingredients

  • 500g beef
  • 6 lasagne sheets
  • Ragù sauce
  • Béchamel sauce
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Sauté beef over high heat and mix beef with ragù sauce
  2. Put meat mixed with sauce in baking tray and cover it with lasagne sheets.
  3. After every meat layer put béchamel sauce.
  4. When tray is filled bake it on 200 degrees celcius for 40 mins.
  5. Finish it by putting parmesan cheese on top.
