Mac and Cheese

Mac and cheese s a rich and creamy dish consisting of macaroni pasta mixed with a cheesy sauce. It's particularly popular in the United States.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Double cream
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Chedar cheese

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta
  2. In the pan on medium high heat mix both cheese with the cream till smooth.
  3. Place pasta in to the baking tray and pour cream and cheese mixture over it.
  4. Bake it for 15 mins.
  5. Finish it with more cheese on top and bake for more 5 mins.
